Brief Description

The purpose of this website is to help aspirants to learn gita by viewing the slokas in sanskrit & english together with their meanings. They can also listen to the chanting of individual slokas.
English version of the slokas & their meanings are attributable to Pujya Guru Sivananda Swamiji of "Divine Life Society".
